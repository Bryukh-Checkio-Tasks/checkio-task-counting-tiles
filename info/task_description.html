Nicola decided to build a round landing zone using the ice square tiles. The landing zone must be circular. But Nicola has to know how many tiles he needed for construction.

The square tile has size 1x1 meter. You need to calculate how many to solid and partial tiles for circle with radius N meters. The center of the circle is at the intersection of tiles. For example: For the circle with radius 2 metres need 4 solid and 12 partial tiles.

Input: Radius of circle, a float

Output: Two numbers of quantities solid and partial tiles as a list, [solid, partial]

Example: 
checkio(2) == [4, 12]
checkio(3) == [16, 20]
checkio(2.1) == [4, 20]
checkio(2.5) == [12, 20]
